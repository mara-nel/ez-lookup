Fighters
Fighters are the characters that form a team. Each fighter is a member of a caste and this determines what roles it can assume, what moves it is allowed to learn, and which castes it is weak or strong against. Most fighters can only learn 2 moves.
Teams
A team consists of either 3 or 5 fighter all of whom hail from different castes.
Damage
A damaging move always does 1/8 or more of a fighter's health, which is the damage recieved from a resisted move. A neutral move is 1/4 and a move a fighter is weak to does 1/2. If a fighter's attack that corresponds to their own caste then there is a same caste attack bonus (SCAB) which doubles the damage done.
Order of a Turn
Switches take place before attacks and in both cases higher castes go first. A fighter can choose to learn one less move and is rewarded the ability to go before the next 5 higher castes (imagine their new caste being 5.5 higher).
